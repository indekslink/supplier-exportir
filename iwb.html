LCD Interactive Whiteboard

LCD Interactive Whiteboard will create a collaborative and interactive atmosphere in the workplaces, where colleagues could work together by casting their devices wirelessly. Except for the easy annotation and crystal clear presentation, it also support video conference helping you collaborate and share smarter and thus the teamwork outcome

Features

UHD Image Display
4K crystal clear display delivers the highest image quality, giving the most fantastic teaching And learning experience.

Multi-user Collaboration
With up to 10 points simultaneous touch, users can do collaborative interactivity in any school or working environment.

Built in Windows Systems, Android
Download all your favorite Apps like you would do on your smartphone and annotate on the screen under any source.

OPS Connection
Plug-n-play detachable OPS module enables optional configurations, which is easier for maintenance and upgrading.

Integrated Loudspeakers
Build in front-facing 2*10w loudspeakers for great listening experience throughout the whole room.

Plug and Play
Quickly connect to multitude of audio or video devices using the front and rear ports.

Integrated Modular Plug-in Box
The Android board, Power supply board and OPS modules are all integrated into the Modular Plug-in Box for fast and convenient upgrading and maintenance.

Front Detachable Design
Infrared touch frame adopts front-facing independent cover design, easy to disassemble the Infrared frame without any tool assist.
